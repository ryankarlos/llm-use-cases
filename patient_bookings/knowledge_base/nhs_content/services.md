# NHS Services Overview

## GP Services

Your GP surgery provides:
- Consultations for health concerns
- Prescriptions and medication reviews
- Vaccinations and immunisations
- Health checks and screening
- Referrals to specialists
- Fit notes (sick notes)
- Travel health advice

## When to See Your GP

See your GP for:
- Ongoing health problems
- New symptoms that concern you
- Medication reviews
- Mental health support
- Preventive care

## When NOT to Use GP

For emergencies, use:
- 999 for life-threatening situations
- A&E for serious injuries
- NHS 111 for urgent but not emergency care

## Specialist Services

Specialists you may be referred to:
- Cardiology (heart)
- Dermatology (skin)
- Gastroenterology (digestive)
- Neurology (brain/nerves)
- Orthopaedics (bones/joints)
- Respiratory (lungs)
- And many more

## NHS 111

Call 111 when:
- You need medical help fast but it's not 999 emergency
- You're not sure what service you need
- You need health information or reassurance

## Pharmacy Services

Your local pharmacy can help with:
- Minor ailments
- Medication advice
- Emergency prescriptions
- Health checks
