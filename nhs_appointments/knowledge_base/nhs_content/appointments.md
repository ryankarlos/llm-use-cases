# NHS Appointments Guide

## Booking an Appointment

### How to Book
- Online through the NHS App or GP surgery website
- By calling your GP surgery
- In person at reception
- Through this booking assistant

### Types of Appointments

**GP Appointments**
- Routine: For non-urgent health concerns (1-2 weeks wait)
- Urgent: Same day for problems needing quick attention
- Telephone: Quick advice, medication reviews

**Specialist Appointments**
- Require GP referral
- Wait times vary by specialty
- You can choose which hospital to attend

### What to Bring
- NHS number (if known)
- List of current medications
- List of symptoms and when they started
- Any relevant test results

## Cancelling or Rescheduling

Please give at least 24 hours notice. This allows the slot to be offered to another patient.

## Out of Hours

When your GP surgery is closed:
- NHS 111: For urgent medical help
- A&E: For life-threatening emergencies only
- 999: For immediate emergencies
